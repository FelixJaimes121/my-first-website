My First Webpage about Cookies and Giraffes

This is the first webpage that I create that is geared towards cookies. There are many types of cookies. For example chocolate and coconut! Be warned, these will fly off of the plate!

Ingredients for Cookies

  • Butter
  • White sugar
  • Brown sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Oatmeal
  • Coconut

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 325.
  2. Beat the butter, sugar, eggs and vanilla together until creamy.
  3. Mix together the flour, baking soda and salt in a separate bowl.
  4. Add flour mixture to butter mixture slowly.
  5. Stir in chocolate chips, oatmeal and coconut.
  6. Bake for 10 minutes or until golden brown.
